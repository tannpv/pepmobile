Annual Membership Meeting and PEP Awards

Join us for PEP’s annual membership meeting that includes elections for the Board of Directors and the awarding of PEP’s Environmental Stewardship Awards to our members who have achieved innovative or significant success in applying environmental best practices to their business or the community. The PEP board also recognizes an annual Community Partner. After the meeting, enjoy a cocktail and delicious food while networking and enjoying the sunset over the Delta. The event takes place on April 16 at 5 Rivers Delta Center. Thank you to our 2013 Event Sponsor Jedson Engineering.

Who are the Partners for Environmental Progress?

We are concerned businesses--both large and small--who value our natural resources and our economy.

Members of PEP believe that the future of our area depends on ensuring a balance between business development, industrial growth and a healthy environment. For future generations to enjoy the natural treasures our area is blessed with, we must have not only a clean environment, but also meaningful and productive job opportunities. We believe that these concepts are completely compatible and we have formed a coalition to create a common voice.

Guiding Principle

Partners for Environmental Progress was founded on the guiding principle of preserving existing business and promoting business growth while enhancing the environment and quality of life in the Mobile/Baldwin area.

Why Should YOU Join Pep?

Partners for Environmental Progress provides an opportunity to join with others in promoting the need for business growth while enhancing the environment and quality of life.

PEP gives you an opportunity to network at our regular meetings with plant managers, purchasing managers, and engineering managers of industrial sites in our area.

You will also receive updates on projects at local industrial sites, information about member companies, and updates on current issues critical to our business.

Core Beliefs

Members of Partners for Environmental Progress share three core beliefs:

  • Clean air and water are paramount to the health
    and prosperity of the Mobile/Baldwin area
  • Information concerning environmental impact must
    be based on facts and on sound scientific principles
  • Business, industry, and the community must work
    together to continuously improve air and water quality in our area

How does it work?

PEP works to bring together people who share our common vision. By combining our efforts, we can ensure that the quality of our environment continues to improve and our economy continues to prosper.
